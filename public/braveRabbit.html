Once upon a time in a lush, green forest, there lived a little rabbit named Rosie. Rosie was known for her bravery and kind heart. One sunny day, Rosie heard a cry for help. She hopped towards the sound and found a baby bird that had fallen out of its nest.

Rosie knew she had to help. She gently picked up the baby bird and climbed the tall tree to place it back in its nest. The mother bird was so grateful that she sang a beautiful song just for Rosie. From that day on, Rosie and the bird family became best friends, and Rosie was known as the bravest rabbit in the forest.
